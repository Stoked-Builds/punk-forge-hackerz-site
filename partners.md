# Partners & Sponsors
Help us build the punk commons. We’re looking for venue hosts, kit fabricators, funders, and storytellers who respect youth leadership and open knowledge.

## What We Offer
- Open-source curriculum + facilitator training packages.
- Kit-as-a-service subscriptions (hardware, maintenance, safety docs).
- Transparent impact + financial reporting.
- Co-designed pilots with laser focus on community need.

## Who We Need
- **Venue Hosts:** Libraries, youth zones, arts centres, colleges.
- **Suppliers/Fabricators:** Electronics shops, printers, makerspaces.
- **Funders:** Councils, foundations, ethical tech companies, community bond buyers.
- **Media & Amplifiers:** Local radio, zines, community platforms.

Request the partnership playbook + engagement scorecards by emailing partnerships@punkforgehackerz.org with your organisation, city, and how you’d like to help.

## Contact
- Partnerships & sponsorships: partnerships@punkforgehackerz.org
- Venue / host enquiries: spaces@punkforgehackerz.org
- Media & storytelling: press@punkforgehackerz.org

We’ll publish an intake form once the data stack is live. For now, email and tell us your city, venue, and what kind of support you can offer.
